Accuracy is easy — and often misleading

MetricAsksWatch out
AccuracyHow often correct?Fails on imbalanced classes
PrecisionOf predicted positives, how many true?False alarms
RecallOf true positives, how many found?Missed rare labels
F1Harmonic mean of P & RBetter default on skewed data
