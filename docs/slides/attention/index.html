Who does "it" refer to?

sentence

the dog chased the cat because it was hungry

Attention lets "it" link back to "dog" — looking at the right place in the sentence.

Q · K · V

Three roles

Q asks "what do I need?", K "what is available?", V "the content". High Q·K match → pull more V from that token.

Self / cross · multi-head

scope

Self vs cross

Self = words in the same sentence look at each other; cross = target sentence looking at the source sentence (eg machine translation).

multi-head

Many perspectives

Each end captures a type of relationship (grammar, topic...) that runs in parallel and then combined.
